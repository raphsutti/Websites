Grace Hopper

The Creator of the World's First Computer Compiler

Hopper with the UNIVAC I console 1960

Timeline

  • 1906: born in New York USA
  • 1944: Hopper and her team developed UNIVAC I
  • 1952: Finished the first compiler written in A0 system
  • 1959: Influenced in machine independent compiler, COBOL
  • 1991: National Medal of Technology awarded to Hopper
  • 1992: Died at the age of 85
  • 2016: Posthumously awarded Presidential Medal of Freedom
